Worldwide Partnership

The WOSTEP Worldwide Partnership is a network of competence centers and watchmaking schools who have entered into a partnership with WOSTEP to educate and provide certified professionals for regional markets. 

Through the provision of standardised educational programs and infrastructure, our Worldwide Partnership schools offer individuals the oppurtunity to obtain an internationally recognised qualification in various regional centres.

The framework of the Worldwide Partnership provides individuals with the unique opportunity to join our industry armed with relevant skills and know-how to enjoy a successful technical career.

All members of the Worldwide Partnership network adhere to strict standards as laid out by WOSTEP.
