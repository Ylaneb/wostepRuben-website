WOSTEP: a unique watchmaking institution

Read more

Founded in 1966, the WOSTEP Foundation is a neutral and independent training center supported by members of the Swiss watch industry. Our mission is to train the next generation of Swiss watchmaking professionals by offering specialized training programs and consulting services.

Trainings

Whether you're a novice watchmaker looking to acquire basic skills, or an experienced professional looking to deepen your knowledge, WOSTEP offers a range of programs to suit your needs.
