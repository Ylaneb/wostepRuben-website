POLISHING – DIFFERENT POLISHING TECHNIQUES IN MANCHESTER

For the first time, a WOSTEP polishing course is being offered at one of our worldwide partnership schools. The 3-week polishing course – Different Polishing Techniques, is now available at the British School of Watchmaking in Manchester, UK. We are very happy to be able to extend the knowledge abroad, thanks to the excellent cooperation with our trusted partnership school in Manchester.

WOSTEP polishing instructor José Berenguer recently accompanied the new BSOW polishing trainer Mr Simon Massey who attended a dedicated training course in Neuchâtel last year.

The atelier at BSOW is equipped with the latest state-of-the-art polishing machines, tools and consumables.

Our congratulations and best wishes to the British School of Watchmaking and Mr Simon Massey for the new course now being offered.

The whole team in Neuchâtel would like to wish you and your students a great summer. Several of our team members have been and are currently at your schools for the Final Examinations. We wish all those taking their Final Examination the best of luck and congratulate those who have already received their certificates.

Have a peaceful and enjoyable summer time!
