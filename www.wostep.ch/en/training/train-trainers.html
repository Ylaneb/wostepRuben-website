Train the Trainers

Didactical and pedagogical development for trainers

A comprehensive course enabling practising watchmakers in charge of training to develop a structured approach and acquire key strategies for effective adult learning.

Training objectives

  • The 2-week WOSTEP Train The Trainer course provides prospective watchmaking instructors and trainers with the necessary competencies and confidence to ensure effective teaching to adult learners.
  • Participants will develop their communication and pedagogical skills and acquire key competencies for successful lesson planning and instruction, as well as evaluating learning outcomes.

Prerequisites

The Train The Trainer course is open to all practicing watchmakers in charge of training, or occupying a position in adult training environments, where communication of technical knowledge is needed (workshop supervisors, training managers). To enrol for the course, there are no mandatory candidate requirements, however it is recommended to have some comprehension of communication and didactics. Candidates who wish to attend are invited to fill out and submit the enclosed preliminary self-assessment sheet when registering for the course.

Program

 First week

  • Communication Basics
  • Public speaking
  • Elements in group dynamics
  • General knowledge on adult learning and training
  • Reflection on planning lessons according to simple didactic models
  • Reflecting on our attitudes and behavior in our role as a teacher

Second week

  • Accurate formulation of educational objectives
  • Adaptation of training contents
  • Simple evaluation methods
  • Development of lesson plans
  • Development of course materials and effective use of media
  • Managing group dynamics and conflicts
  • Assessment of learning
