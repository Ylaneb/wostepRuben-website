Program

Participants will learn different preperation techniques such as grinding with grinding wheels and felting. These techniques, while mainly applicable to round-shaped pieces, will also allow you to work on flat parts. 

Theoretical instruction and practical exercices on case middle, case back, bezel and bracelet.
